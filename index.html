Research interests

I am interested in designing nanoscale biomaterials with a desired function or property. The design process is challenging because we do not want to find properties of a given material (forward design); instead, we want to find new materials with a desired property (inverse design). I approach these problems using both rational design, which involves creating materials with similar structures or chemical compositions to a known material with the desired property, and data-driven methods, which often allow us to efficiently explore the chemical space and find new materials by identifying expressive descriptors. Here are some relevant publications on this topic: ChemRxiv (03/2023), ChemRxiv (02/2023), ACS Food Sci. Technol. (2022), J. Chem. Phys. (2021), Adv. Func. Mater. (2019)
