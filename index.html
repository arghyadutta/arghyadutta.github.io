Welcome to my home page!

In my research I use mathematical and computational tools to design a variety of materials, especially functional biomaterials. The main tools I employ are statistical mechanics, molecular simulations, rational design, and data-driven methods. I am especially interested in utilizing data-driven machine learning methods because they enable efficient exploration of the chemical space and offer insight by identifying expressive descriptors. Some relevant papers: ChemRxiv (03/2023), ChemRxiv (02/2023), ACS Food Sci. Technol. (2022), J. Chem. Phys. (2021), Adv. Func. Mater. (2019)
