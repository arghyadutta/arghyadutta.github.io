Arghya Dutta

I'm a physicist with a keen interest in biomaterial design. Because expressive descriptors can ease material design, I focus on developing data-driven methods to identify them as sequence patterns, equations, or reduced vector embeddings. To this end, I use mathematical and computational techniques, borrowing from machine learning, simulations, and statistical mechanics.

I work as a postdoctoral researcher at Goethe University.
