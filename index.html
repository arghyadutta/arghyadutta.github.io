Arghya Dutta

I'm a physicist with a keen interest in biomaterial design. Since expressive descriptors ease such design, in my research, I try to identify them using data-driven methods—often borrowing techniques from machine learning, numerical simulations, and statistical mechanics. The descriptors can come in various forms, too—they can be sequence patterns, equations, or reduced vector embeddings. And they do help design better materials, as our colleagues have experimentally demonstrated. It's exciting, have a look!

I work as a postdoctoral researcher at Goethe University. [CV, Twitter, Google Scholar, Email: argphy@gmail.com]
