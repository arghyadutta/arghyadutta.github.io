Welcome to my home page!

Research interests

In my research I use mathematical and computational tools—statistical mechanics, molecular simulations, rational design, and machine learning—to design a variety of functional biomaterials. To this end, I especially like to develop and utilize data-driven machine-learning methods to explore chemical space and identify expressive descriptors.

Some relevant papers: Nature Communications (accepted, 2023; preprint), Biomaterials Science (2023), ACS Food Science & Technology (2022), Journal of Chemical Physics (2021), and Advanced Functional Materials (2019)
