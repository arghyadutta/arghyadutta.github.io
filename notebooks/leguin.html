Ursula K. Le Guin

Recommended

Guin, U. K. L. (2016). A Wizard of Earthsea. Puffin.
Le Guin's prose flows, interlaced with intricate, compassionate thoughts.
Guin, U. K. L. (2018). The Books of Earthsea. Gollancz.
Complete Earthsea series. Amazon reviews say this edition is slightly unwieldy, but I couldn't find any other edition. There is a collection of first four books, but then you need the rest separately, which is mildly annoying.
