Protein $P–T$ phase diagrams

I came to know about protein's interesting phase diagrams from a YouTube course by Ali Hassanali (ICTP). For some examples, see Hawley (1971), Asherie (2004), and Gasic et al. (2019).

Hawley, S. A. Reversible Pressure-Temperature Denaturation of Chymotrypsinogen. Biochemistry 1971, 10 (13), 2436–2442.
Asherie, N. Protein Crystallization and Phase Diagrams. Methods 2004, 34 (3), 266–272.
Gasic, A. G.; Boob, M. M.; Prigozhin, M. B.; Homouz, D.; Daugherty, C. M.; Gruebele, M.; Cheung, M. S. Critical Phenomena in the Temperature-Pressure-Crowding Phase Diagram of a Protein. Phys. Rev. X 2019, 9 (4), 041035.
