Artificial Intelligence

Status of AI

  • The Limits of AI by Hubert Dreyfus (1985) [Dreyfus lays down the core objections against AGI in a slightly acerbic tone.]
  • Why General Artificial Intelligence Will Not Be Realized by R. Fjelland (2020), Humanities and Social Sciences Communications, 7 (1), 10. [Compelling and persuasive article against AGI.]
  • A Short Introduction to Artificial Intelligence: Methods, Success Stories, and Current Limitations by Heitzinger and Woltran (2024). From "Introduction to Digital Humanism: A Textbook", Springer, pp. 135–149. [Excellent and up-to-date broad overview of AI.]
  • "Why the Godfather of A.I. Fears What He’s Built", Geoffrey Hinton's profile by the New Yorker (2023) [Useful read, despite the dramatic title.]
  • Ethics of Artificial Intelligence and Robotics at the Stanford Enclycopeida of Philosophy. [Broad yet deep, with numerous references.]
