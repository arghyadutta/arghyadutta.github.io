Quantum Optics

Recommended

Prof. Amarenda Kumar Sarma's (IIT Guwahati) lecture series: Essentials of Quantum Optics
If the lectures feel a bit slow and tedious at times, that's only because he derived almost everything he talked about. Strong recommendations!
F. Campaioli, J. H. Cole, and H. Hapuarachchi, Quantum Master Equations: Tips and Tricks for Quantum Optics, Quantum Computing, and Beyond, PRX Quantum 5, 020202 (2024).
Tangentially connected, but very useful.
Mikhail Lukin, Lecture Notes Quantum Optics (2021).
N. Meher, Quantum optics in MATLAB, J. Phys. B: At. Mol. Opt. Phys. 57, 073001 (2024).
Good introduction to numerical side of quantum optics.
C. Navarrete-Benlloch, Introduction to Quantum Optics, arXiv:2203.13206.
