Socialism

Recommended

Gellately, R. (2008). Lenin, Stalin, and Hitler: The Age of Social Catastrophe (Reprint edition). Vintage.
Arrow, K. J. (1978). A Cautious Case for Socialism. Dissent Magazine
Arrow offers a careful analysis of capitalism and socialism, without asking you to join a camp.
Mommsen, P. (2025, March 3). The Quest to Emancipate Labor. Plough
Not entirely on socialism, but it touches on Marx's alienation. I think the author is hopelessly romantic—but then what’s life if one can't even dream?
Edwards, J., & Leiter, B. (2025). Marx. Routledge, Taylor & Francis group.
Russell, B. (1956). Why I am Not a Communist. In Portraits from Memory. https://www.rjgeib.com/thoughts/opiate/why.html
Gilabert, P., & O’Neill, M. (2024). Socialism. In E. N. Zalta & U. Nodelman (Eds.). The Stanford Encyclopedia of Philosophy
Thorough summary; recommended.
