Well-written books on science and scientists

Books that I think are well-written.
Also, I've added some other books—which I've not read—but was suggested by Twitter users; it started when I asked on Twitter which books people like for clarity and prose.
If available, I have added links to Open Library, where you can borrow and read quite a few of them for free!
A helpful video by Meghan Adrian on how Open Library works and how you can browse and, if available, borrow books.
